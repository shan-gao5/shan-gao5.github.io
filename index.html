About

I'm a masters student in computer science at McGill University under the supervision of Professor Luc Devroye, currently working on the properties of degree-restricted uniform attachment trees. Previously, I was an undergraduate student at McGill University where I worked on statistical learning theory with Professor Hamed Hatami and Pooya Hatami, specifically on questions involving differential privacy, stable learning, communication complexity and combinatorics. A long time ago, I started my undergraduate degree in Physics where I did research on high-energy astrophysics. Thanks to my wonderful family, friends and colleagues, I'm still hanging in there.
